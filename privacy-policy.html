Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 25, 2026

Welcome to Expenser. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data within the application.

1. Data Collection and Storage

Expenser is designed as an offline-first application. All financial records, profiles, and expense data you enter are stored locally on your device's internal storage using a secure SQLite database.

  • We do not collect, transmit, or store your financial data on any external servers.
  • Your data remains under your total control and stays on your device.

2. Permissions Used

To provide a seamless experience, the app requires the following permissions:

  • SMS Permission: Used solely to detect transaction alerts from banks to help you log expenses automatically. We do not read personal messages.
  • Storage Access: Used only to save and manage the local database of your expenses.

3. Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs that collect personal identification information.

4. Data Security

Since the data is stored locally, the security of your information depends on the security of your mobile device. We recommend using device-level encryption and screen locks to protect your data.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the "Last Updated" date will be revised accordingly.
